Commercial French Polishing Services

Surface Damage Repair

We provide professional damage repair and restoration services for a wide range of surfaces in residential, commercial, and high-end properties. Our skilled technicians carry out seamless repairs, restoring damaged areas to their original appearance while avoiding the cost and disruption of full replacement.

What services we provide

Marble & Natural Stone – Chips, cracks, scratches, edge damage, blemishes, polishing, and colour matching.
Glass – Scratch removal, surface restoration, and polishing for architectural and decorative glass.
Tiles & Ceramics – Repairs to chipped, cracked, and damaged wall and floor tiles with expert colour matching.
Stainless Steel – Scratch removal, dent repairs, polishing, and refinishing to restore the original finish.
Wood & Timber – Repairs to doors, flooring, furniture, joinery, and architectural woodwork, including scratches, dents, burns, and impact damage.
Plastic & Composite Surfaces – Restoration of damaged uPVC, laminates, acrylics, and other plastic surfaces.
Metal Surfaces – Repairs to aluminium, steel, brass, bronze, and coated metals, including scratches, dents, and surface defects.
